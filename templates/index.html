About US

Ankura aims to harbour the talents of young minds and give them an insight to the real world. It's the annual even semester technical fest. Of the students, by the students and for the students. This comes under the IEEE Student Chapter of the National Institute of Engineering, Mysuru. Students from all over the nation take active participation every year. Technology being the main focus, this event tends to open the conclaves of the youth and get those aspiring ideas and thoughts, bringing them for the use of greater good. Ankura is a Sanskrit word that means sprouting. And that is something we intend to do in our events. We intend to ‘ankura’ the minds of all the participants in a certain technically defined ways that shall help the students in their life for a long run. Innovation has always been the way Ankura works. With the best of minds from around the country, we strive to steer people towards a more open and thoughtful society. To disabuse from the delusions of fog, Ankura has done a tremendous job over the years by engaging young minds in technical activities, and training them for real-time management skills. Mark your calendar! We welcome you to witness Ankura this March of 2017 and indulge yourself in the world of our technical and management skills enhancement activities. These are the things that go deep within people,and we make sure that they go forward with you. Let’s travel at the speed of life, let’s Ankura!
